Cookie Policy

Updated October 6th, 2023

Trongital is dedicated to ensuring transparency and clarity regarding the utilization of cookies and related technologies on our website, which can be accessed at www.trongital.com. As part of our ongoing commitment to privacy and data protection, we invite you to explore the details of our cookie usage, its purpose, and your control options. Within this policy, you'll discover the types of cookies we employ, their functions, and how you can manage them to tailor your browsing experience to your liking.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies, in essence, are compact text files that are deposited on your device during your visits to websites. These unobtrusive data snippets serve a multitude of purposes in the digital realm, including enhancing website functionality, retaining user preferences, scrutinizing website traffic patterns, and customizing content to align with your individual interests and browsing habits.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the proper functioning of our website and enable core features such as security, authentication, and accessibility. They cannot be disabled.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website, allowing us to enhance its performance and user experience. They collect anonymous data and are used for statistical analysis.
  • Functionality Cookies: Functionality cookies enable us to remember your preferences (such as language or region) and provide enhanced features. These cookies may be set by third-party providers.
  • Marketing/Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver personalized content and advertisements based on your interests and online behavior. They may also be set by third-party advertising networks.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To improve website functionality and performance.
  • To remember your preferences and settings.
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior.
  • To deliver personalized content and advertisements.
  • To monitor and enhance the security of our website.

Cookie Management

Managing cookies is within your control through your browser settings. The majority of web browsers provide options to block or delete cookies, restrict their use to specific websites, or issue notifications when a website attempts to place a cookie on your device. For detailed guidance on managing cookies, please consult your browser's help menu. It is important to be aware, though, that disabling certain cookies may have an effect on the functionality and overall user experience of our website. Therefore, we recommend making informed choices when managing cookies to ensure that your experience remains as seamless and enjoyable as possible while using our platform.

Third-Party Cookies

Certain cookies on our website may be established by third-party providers, including analytics or advertising partners. These third-party entities may collect information about your online activities, spanning an extended period and across various websites. It's important to note that we do not exercise control over these third-party cookies. Their usage is contingent upon the privacy policies and cookie policies established by the respective providers. To gain deeper insights into how these providers handle your data, we strongly advise reviewing their policies, which are readily available and accessible on their respective platforms. This will equip you with a more comprehensive understanding of how your information is managed within the broader online ecosystem.

Changes to This Cookie Policy

Our commitment to transparency extends to our Cookie Policy, and as such, we may periodically update it to reflect alterations in our use of cookies or to align with evolving legal obligations. In the event of substantial changes to this policy that could impact your cookie-related experience, we will make sure to inform you by prominently posting a notice on our website. This will serve as a clear and noticeable indication of any noteworthy modifications, ensuring that you are always well-informed regarding our cookie practices.

Contact Us

We value your feedback and are here to provide clarifications or address any issues you may have concerning our cookie practices. By continuing to use our website, you indicate your consent to the use of cookies in accordance with the terms outlined in this Cookie Policy. Your use of our website is governed by these principles, and we are committed to ensuring that your online experience with us is as informative and enjoyable as possible.
