Privacy Policy

Updated October 6th, 2023

At Trongital, we are firmly dedicated to ensuring the utmost protection and security of your personal information. Through this all-encompassing Privacy Policy, our primary objective is to furnish you with an extensive insight into our approach towards managing, collecting, employing, disclosing, and safeguarding your personal data across a multitude of scenarios. This includes instances where you interact with our website, which can be found at www.trongital.com, or when you avail yourself of our wide-ranging services.

Information We Collect

In the pursuit of enhancing your experience and delivering our services effectively, we may gather several categories of personal information, which include: Contact Information: This encompasses details such as your name, email address, phone number, and postal address.

  • Log Data: We may collect information regarding your interactions with our website, such as your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and timestamps.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: To better comprehend your browsing behavior and preferences, we employ cookies and similar tracking technologies.
  • User Account Data: If you choose to create an account with us, we may extend our data collection to encompass additional information, including your username, password, and profile details.

How We Use Your Information

Your personal information serves various essential purposes within our operations, including:

  • Provision and Maintenance of Services: We utilize your data to ensure the delivery and maintenance of our services.
  • Enhancement of User Experience and Website: Personalization and continuous improvement of our website experience.
  • Communication: We may employ your data to keep you updated through newsletters, marketing communications, and timely responses to inquiries, comments, or questions.
  • Legal Compliance: In adherence to legal obligations, we may process your information as required.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal information in specific instances, which include:

  • Service Providers and Partners: Collaboration with service providers and partners to facilitate the delivery of our services.
  • Legal Authorities: Sharing information with legal authorities when necessitated by legal obligations or to safeguard our rights.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of significant business changes, such as mergers, acquisitions, or sales, your information may be shared.

Your Choices

We respect your rights concerning your personal information:

  • Access, Update, or Deletion: If applicable, you can access, update, or delete your personal information by logging into your account.
  • Marketing Communication Opt-Out: You have the option to unsubscribe from our marketing communications by following the provided instructions.
  • Cookie Management: Adjust your browser settings to disable cookies; however, this may affect your website experience.

Data Security

Our dedication is unwavering when it comes to the implementation of robust security measures designed to protect your valuable personal data. We take every reasonable precaution to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your information. However, it is essential to recognize and understand that despite our best efforts and the advanced security protocols we employ, no method of data transmission over the internet can offer an absolute guarantee of security.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

Our commitment to your privacy is an ongoing responsibility, and as such, our Privacy Policy undergoes periodic updates to stay in harmony with evolving best practices and to ensure strict compliance with legal requirements. In the event of substantial changes to this policy, we will take steps to inform you promptly. This notification may occur through email, ensuring that you are directly informed of the alterations.
Additionally, we may choose to prominently display a notice on our website, making it easily accessible to all users. These measures are in place to keep you well-informed about any modifications to our privacy practices and to maintain the transparency that is central to our commitment to safeguarding your privacy and personal information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, inquiries, or concerns regarding our Privacy Policy or the management of your personal information, we encourage you to get in touch with us. You can reach us at hello@trongital.com. We value your privacy and are here to address any queries or issues you may have to ensure that your personal data is handled in a manner that aligns with your expectations and our commitment to privacy and security.
